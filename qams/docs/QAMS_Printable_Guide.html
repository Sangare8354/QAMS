Teacher Workflow

  1. Login with your credentials
  2. Select course from dashboard
  3. Fill QAMS form with all required data
  4. Upload required PDF documents
  5. Submit for review
  6. Wait for approval or correction request
  7. If rejected, correct and resubmit

Reviewer Workflow (Dept Head, Dean, QAMS Head)

  1. Login to your role-specific panel
  2. View pending submissions
  3. Review form details and PDF documents
  4. Make decision:
    • Approve - Forward to next level
    • Revert - Return with correction reason
  5. Continue until final approval
