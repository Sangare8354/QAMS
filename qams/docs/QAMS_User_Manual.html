1. System Overview

What is QAMS?

QAMS (Quality Assurance Management System) is a web-based platform designed to streamline the quality assurance process for educational institutions. It enables teachers to submit course quality forms, and allows Department Heads, Deans, and QAMS Heads to review, approve, and archive these forms through a structured workflow.

Key Benefits

  • Streamlined Process: Automated approval workflow from teacher to archive
  • Transparency: Complete audit trail of all actions
  • Accountability: Clear role-based responsibilities
  • Efficiency: Reduce paper work and manual tracking
  • Compliance: Meet accreditation requirements with proper documentation

Workflow Overview

Teacher → Department Head → Dean → QAMS Head → Archive

Submit → Approve/Revert → Approve/Revert → Final Approve → Store

10. Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: How do I know if my form was approved?
A: You'll receive a notification (bell icon turns red) and the status on your dashboard will change from "Pending" to "Approved".

Q: Can I edit a form after submission?
A: No, once submitted, forms are locked until rejected for corrections. You must wait for the reviewer to revert it.

Q: What happens if my form is rejected?
A: You'll receive the rejection reason and can edit and resubmit the corrected form.

Q: How long does the approval process take?
A: Depends on Department Head, Dean, and QAMS Head review times. You'll be notified at each step.

Q: Can I view archived forms?
A: Yes, approved forms are visible in the QAMS Head archive section. Teachers can view their approved forms.

Technical Questions

Q: What if I forget my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" to request reset from administrator, or contact your system administrator directly.

Q: How do I change my password?
A: Go to Settings → Security tab → Enter current and new password → Update Password.

Q: What file formats are accepted for uploads?
A: Only PDF files, maximum size 10MB. Files must be clear and readable.

Q: Can I see who approved my form?
A: Yes, the approval chain in the form details shows all approvers and dates.

Q: How do I know if I have pending tasks?
A: Check your dashboard for pending forms and the notification bell for alerts.

Workflow Questions

Q: What is the complete approval flow?
A: Teacher submits → Department Head approves → Dean approves → QAMS Head final approves → Archive

Q: What happens at each rejection level?
A:

  • Dept Head reject → Teacher corrects and resubmits
  • Dean reject → Dept Head reviews and forwards to Teacher
  • QAMS Head reject → Dean reviews and cascades down

Q: Can I request a resubmission?
A: Yes, reviewers can "Revert" forms with a reason, allowing the submitter to correct and resubmit.

Q: What happens to rejected forms?
A: Rejected forms remain in the system with the rejection reason visible, allowing correction and resubmission.

Admin Questions

Q: How do I add a new user?
A: Go to Admin → User Management → Add New User → Fill details → Save.

Q: How do I set up the hierarchy?
A: Go to Admin → Hierarchy Configuration → Add mapping for each teacher.

Q: How do I create a new academic year?
A: Go to Admin → Academic Configuration → Add New Academic Year → Fill dates.

Q: How often should I backup the database?
A: Daily backups are recommended. Configure automatic backups in Backup Management.
